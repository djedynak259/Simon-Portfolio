Simon is a San Francisco based artist, painter, and creator. His work centers around pop-culture, outer-space, and patterns. He loves noodle soups.

Simon uses spray paint, paint pens, acrylic paint, stickers, and whatever he can get his hands on to create his art.

He grew up in Santa Barbara, California and attended Cal-Poly San Luis Obispo.

Contact

Simon is available for local projects, collaborations, gallery or venue showings as well as live paintings and commissioned pieces. Use the form to inquire about rates and availability, or just to say hi.
